About

I'm a Ph.D. candidate in the Computer Science department at Rutgers University. My research is at the intersection of Programming Languages, Software Engineering and High Performance Computing. I am interested in building tools using program analysis techniques that aid the development and debugging of parallel programs. My current work involves developing programming language abstractions and tools to pinpoint performance issues in parallel programs. I work with Professor Santosh Nagarakatte in the RAPL group.
